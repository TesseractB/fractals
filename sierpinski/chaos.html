The making of a Sierpinski Triangle using the "chaos game"

The rules of the game are simple:

  1. Draw the 3 corners of an equilateral triangle
  2. Pick a random point inside the triangle. This will be known as your current position
  3. Draw a point halfway between your current position and one of the first 3 points, picked at random.
